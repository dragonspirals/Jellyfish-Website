Fun Facts about Jellyfish

Jellyfish are 95% water

Only 5% of a jellyfish's body is made of solid matter.


Jellyfish do not have bones, brains, lungs, blood or a heart

Jellyfish have a basic nervous system, and a digestive system, but not much else


Some Jellyfish can age backwards!

The hydrozoan Turritopsis dohrnii, an animal about 4.5 millimetres wide and tall (likely making it smaller than the nail on your little finger), can actually reverse its life cycle. It has been dubbed the immortal jellyfish.

A jellyfish's mouth is also their anus

Okay, this is a bit misleading as jellyfish don't technically have a mouth or anus. They instead have one opening that both ingests and discards waste
